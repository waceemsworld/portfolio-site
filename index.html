Scaling an owner-led support agency

What I did: Introduced a CRM for operations and ClickUp for task visibility and KPIs, built real operational structure, and set up weekly management standups and monthly agent sessions.

ClickUp + Discord automation

Challenge: An Amazon brand needed a streamlined task system connected to their team's Discord channel, and to move task management off Google Sheets into ClickUp.
